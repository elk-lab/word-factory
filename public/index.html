          Word Factory Mechanics

          • Board is 5x5, including occasional Qu tiles.
          • Tap tiles or press and trace to build a word path.
          • Words must use touching tiles in any direction.
          • The same tile cannot be reused in one word.
          • Release after tracing to auto-submit the word.
          • Words used reset every new round.
          • Scoring: 3-4 letters = 1, 5 = 2, 6 = 3, 7 = 5, 8+ = 11.
