        Word Factory Mechanics

        • Board is 5x5, including occasional Qu tiles.
        • Build words using adjacent tiles (8 directions).
        • You cannot reuse the same tile in one word.
        • Words are validated with US English dictionary rules.
        • Words used reset every new round.
        • Scoring: 3-4 letters = 1, 5 = 2, 6 = 3, 7 = 5, 8+ = 11.
